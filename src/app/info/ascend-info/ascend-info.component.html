Ascension

Every 20 Total Prestige you can ascend. Ascension reset Prestige to 1 but you will get a 2*Ascension_Level multiplier to your prestige skills and increase Automator Level by Ascension_Level*30%

  • Ascension 0 Starting Level, Prestige effect: 100% (normal)
  • Ascension 1 Level Required: 20, Prestige effect: 200%
  • Ascension 2 Level Required: 40, Prestige effect: 400%
  • Ascension 3 Level Required: 60, Prestige effect: 600%
  • Ascension 4 Level Required: 80, Prestige effect: 800%
