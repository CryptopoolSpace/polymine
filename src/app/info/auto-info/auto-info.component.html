Automators

Automators are unlocked based upon your Automator Level, which you can see in the Automators tab: Automators
"Automator Level" is calculated by Prestige*(1+(0.3*Ascension_Level))
Automators can automate some aspects of the game, such as searching for enemies.

Automators Stats
  • Active: On Turn Automator on or off
  • Active: When not have Robot Factory Turn Automator off if you have at least one Robot Factory
  • Interval Minimum interval, Lower intervals are unlocked by raising your Automator Level. You should put higher interval on low priority automators to avoid Priority inversion
  • Priority Higher number equal higher priority
  • Res % Percent of resources to use. With 100% automators will activate as soon as you have 100% of the resources it requires.
